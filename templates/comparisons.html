About Comparisons
Understanding the Comparison

Comparisons help you evaluate the accuracy of your predictions by comparing them with experimental results. The system calculates:

  • Difference: The absolute difference between the predicted and experimental values.
  • Percent Error: The percentage difference relative to the experimental value.

The comparison is color-coded based on the percent error:

  • Good Less than 5% error
  • Warning 5-10% error
  • Bad More than 10% error

Note: Comparisons are only available for properties that have both predictions and experimental results. If you don't see a property in the dropdown, you may need to add a prediction or record an experiment first.
